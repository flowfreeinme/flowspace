Where does onboarding appear?

New users need to pick their starter path — where does that choice happen?

A

Full-screen takeover

After email confirmation, the user lands on a full-screen "Welcome to Flowspace" page before seeing the app at all. They pick their path, workspace populates, then they drop into the app. Clean, focused, no distractions.

B

Modal on first load

The app loads normally with the default workspace in the background, and a centered modal pops up asking the user to choose a path. They can dismiss it to skip. Less jarring, but the empty default workspace is visible behind it.

C

Setup card on the home screen

A dismissible "Get started" card appears as a widget on the home screen dashboard. The user can click it to pick a path anytime, or ignore it. Lowest friction — the app is immediately usable — but easy to miss or dismiss.
