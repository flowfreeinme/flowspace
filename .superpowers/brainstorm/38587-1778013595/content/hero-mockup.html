What goes in the hero product preview?

The big visual below the headline — most important element on the page

A

Real screenshot

Actual app UI, browser-framed. Most trustworthy — visitors see the real product. Requires a good-looking board to screenshot.

B

Upgraded illustrated mockup

Keep the current HTML mockup approach but make it much richer — more realistic cards, sections, presence avatars, AI panel visible. Full control, no dependency on a real screenshot.

C

Split: desktop + mobile

Show the desktop app on the left, the mobile UI on the right. Sells the cross-device story and highlights the new mobile work.

D

Multi-feature scroll

Three smaller previews instead of one big one — Board view, Calendar view, AI panel. Shows depth without a single screenshot.
